Privacy Policy for Xbout
Last Updated: Nov. 2025
1. Data Collection
Xbout does not transmit any user data to external servers. All data processing happens locally on your browser.
2. Authentication
The extension utilizes your existing session cookies with x.com to fetch public account information. We do not store, log, or share your credentials.
3. Local Storage
We use your browser's Local Storage to cache account information (location, registration year) to reduce network requests. This data resides only on your device.
4. Third-Party Services
This extension interacts directly with x.com APIs. Please refer to X's privacy policy regarding their data handling.
